Chicken and Waffles

Description:

A playful and delightful variation for your lunch or dinner. Crispy breaded chicken paired harmoniously with your preferred waffle selection, all elegantly drizzled with the richness of maple syrup.

While its origins remain a topic of lively debate, it appears that the delightful combination of chicken and waffles first emerged in Harlem during the 1930s. Some attribute it to soul food, while others simply revel in its undeniable and mouthwatering deliciousness.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless chicken thighs
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 cups of all purpose flour
  • 3 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 and a half cups of buttermilk
  • 1 tbsp of hot sauce
  • Canola or vegetable oil
  • Maple syrup
  • Pickle slices

These ingredients are primarly for the chicken, feel free to use any waffles such as your own homemade ones or even microwable options that can be purchased at the store.

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken into generous strips and gently pat them dry using a paper towel
  2. Transfer the chicken to a bowl, generously season with 1 tbsp of kosher salt and 1 tsp of black pepper
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and allow the chicken to marinate for a delightful two hours
  4. Prepare a spacious bowl for crafting the enticing marinade
  5. Gather essential ingredients: buttermilk, eggs, a dash of hot sauce, versatile all-purpose flour, aromatic garlic powder, and a pinch of salt
  6. Skillfully whisk together these harmonious elements to create a captivating marinade
  7. Indulge each piece of chicken in a delicate dance, first in a graceful coating of flour, then a luxurious dip into the marinade, and finally a finale in the flour embrace
  8. Embrace a sturdy, deep pan with about 2 inches of oil, ready for the culinary performance
  9. Warm the stage to an inviting 350°F, creating the perfect environment for the upcoming sizzle
  10. Let the chicken take center stage, sizzling and crisping to a captivating golden brown, achieving a melodious internal temperature of 160°F
  11. With warm waffles as your canvas, allow the masterpiece of fried chicken to elegantly grace its presence
  12. Invite a drizzle of rich maple syrup to elegantly cascade, adding a symphony of flavors to your creation
  13. Should your culinary desires harmonize, consider adding the delightful touch of pickles to this gourmet composition
