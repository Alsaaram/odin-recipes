Chicken and Waffles

Description:

A fun twist to your dinner or lunchtime meal. Breaded chicken with your choice of waffles with a drizzle of maple syrup.

Although widely disputed, it seems that chicken and waffles began to first appear in Harlem during the 1930's. Some label it as a soul food while others just say it's downright delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless chicken thighs
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 cups of all purpose flour
  • 3 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 and a half cups of buttermilk
  • 1 tbsp of hot sauce
  • Canola or vegetable oil
  • Maple syrup
  • Pickle slices

These ingredients are primarly for the chicken, feel free to use any waffles such as your own homemade ones or even microwable options that can be purchased at the store.

Steps:

  1. Cut the chicken into thick strips and dry with a paper towel
  2. Place chicken into a bowl with 1 tbsp kosher salt and 1 tsp black pepper
  3. Cover with plastic wrap and let it sit for two hours
  4. Grab a large bowl to make the marinade
  5. You need buttermilk, eggs, hot sauce, all purpose flower, garlic power and salt
  6. Wisk all these ingredients together
  7. Dip each piece of chicken in the flower, then marinade, then flower again
  8. Fill a heavy bottom pan with about 2 inches of oil
  9. Heat the oil to about 350 F
  10. Fry the chicken until it's golden brown and has an internal temp of 160 F
  11. Grab the warm waffles and place the chicken on them
  12. Drizzle with maple syrup and feel free to add pickles
