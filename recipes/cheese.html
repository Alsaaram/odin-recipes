Grilled cheese

Description:

A tasty snack which will take you back to your childhood. Grilled cheeses are easy and quick to make which is why they're a perfect snack for whenever you might be feeling hungry. Toasted bread with a warm cheesy center to stimulate the tastebuds.

Nobody really knows when the grilled cheese was first created, but its evident that it started becoming extremely popular during the 1920's when America was undergoing its great depression. The use of easy to obtain goods such as cheese and bread is the exact reason why it became a staple throughout the country.

Ingredients:

  • Plain white bread, substitutions can be made for other types of bread
  • Cheese, prefererably cheddar or american but pepper jack and gouda work as well
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Butter the bread on one side
  2. Place butter side down on a hot skillet
  3. Put cheese on top of the bread
  4. Butter another piece of bread and place it butter side up on the cheese
  5. Wait till one side of the bread is lightly brown and repeat with the other side
  6. Continue until the cheese is melted
