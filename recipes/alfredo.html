Chicken Alfredo

Description:

A classic pasta dish people of all ages have enjoyed since its creation in 1908.

Made in Rome by a man named Alfredo di Lelio, it has been said that he created this dish for his wife who lost her appetite after giving birth.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Shredded parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh garlic
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Heavy cream
  • Unsalted butter

Steps:

  1. Boil water and season with salt
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  3. Cook chicken through and add garlic during the last minute
  4. Cook pasta and save pasta water for the sauce
  5. Simmer and reduce cream for about 5 minutes, stir lightly
  6. Add butter to cream and stir untill melted
  7. Place parmesan and pasta with a 1/4 cup of saved pasta water into cream
  8. Season again with salt and pepper
  9. Put in the cooked chicken and thin with pasta water as needed
  10. Serve with garnish
