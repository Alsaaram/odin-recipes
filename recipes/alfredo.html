Chicken Alfredo

Description:

An enduring favorite among people of all ages, this classic pasta dish has been delighting taste buds since its inception in 1908.

Crafted in the heart of Rome by a culinary visionary named Alfredo di Lelio, the origins of this dish trace back to a heartfelt gesture. It's been recounted that Alfredo concocted this creation for his wife, who had lost her appetite following childbirth.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Shredded parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh garlic
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Heavy cream
  • Unsalted butter

Steps:

  1. Begin by boiling water and delicately seasoning it with a touch of salt.
  2. Tenderly coat the chicken with a gentle sprinkle of salt and pepper.
  3. Skillfully cook the chicken until perfectly done, adding the aromatic garlic during the final minute.
  4. Effortlessly prepare the pasta and remember to reserve a quarter cup of the pasta water for the divine sauce.
  5. Gently simmer and artfully reduce the cream for approximately five minutes, occasionally giving it a graceful stir.
  6. Introduce the butter into the cream, stirring with finesse until a harmonious melt is achieved.
  7. Gracefully combine the parmesan, pasta, and a quarter cup of the reserved pasta water into the exquisite cream.
  8. Once more, grace the creation with a subtle symphony of salt and pepper.
  9. Integrate the beautifully cooked chicken into the ensemble and adjust the consistency with pasta water as desired.
  10. Present this masterpiece adorned with a final flourish of garnish.
